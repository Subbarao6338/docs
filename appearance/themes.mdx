## Material Expressive Elements

Knowledge Hub introduces **Material Expressive** elements, featuring highly rounded containers, vibrant tonal palettes, and fluid animations.

### Colorful Pills
Use pills to categorize content or highlight status. They automatically adapt to light and dark modes using Material 3 tonal containers.

<div className="flex flex-wrap gap-3 my-6">
  <span className="pill pill-primary">Primary</span>
  <span className="pill pill-green">Success</span>
  <span className="pill pill-purple">Creative</span>
  <span className="pill pill-orange">Warning</span>
  <span className="pill pill-red">Critical</span>
</div>

```html
<span className="pill pill-primary">Primary</span>
<span className="pill pill-green">Success</span>
```

## Theme Customization

You can switch between light and dark modes, and the accent colors will adapt accordingly to maintain accessibility and visual harmony.

### Light Mode
Optimized for readability with high contrast and subtle grid backgrounds.

### Dark Mode
A sleek, modern aesthetic that reduces eye strain in low-light environments.
